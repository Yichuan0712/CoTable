Ketamine Pharmacokinetic Parameters

The pharmacokinetic parameters of ketamine in children are listed in Table 3. Ketamine is characterized by large distribution volume at steady state and fast clearance. The high clearance rate resulted in a short half-life and a mean residence time.

Table 3. Ketamine Estimated Pharmacokinetic Parameters in Children Population (Values are Mean ± SD)
Parameter Unit Value
Total area under curve Mg/min/L 91 ± 30
Maximum concentration mg/L 1.6 ± 0.68
Total body clearance L/min 0.52 ± 0.26
Body weight normalized clearance L/kg/min 0.025 ± 0.008
Mean residence time hr 2.3 ± 0.64
Distribution volume at steady state L 69 ± 39
Body weight normalized distribution volume L/kg 3.3 ± 1.3
Elimination half-life hr 2.6 ± 1
